BMW M2cs

The BMW M2cs is a high-performance track-focused evolution of the G87-generation M2. It features a 3.0-litre S58 twin-turbocharged inline six engine producing 523 horsepower and 479 Ib-ft of torque. To enhance its performance, BMW utilized lightweight carbon fiber construction, reducing its weight by approxmatately 30 kilograms compared to a standard model.
